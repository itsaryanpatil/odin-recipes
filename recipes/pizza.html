🍕 Chapter 1.1: What is Margherita Pizza?

Margherita pizza is a classic Neapolitan pizza known for its simplicity and balance. It includes:

  • Fresh tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

The colors represent the Italian flag 🇮🇹

🍕 Chapter 1.2: History of Margherita Pizza

This pizza dates back to 1889 when Queen Margherita of Savoy visited Naples. Chef Raffaele Esposito created this pizza using ingredients that reflected the Italian flag. She loved it — and thus, the Margherita pizza was born.

🍕 Chapter 1.3: What Makes a Margherita Pizza?

Component Description
🍞 Dough Neapolitan style with flour, water, yeast, salt
🍅 Sauce San Marzano tomatoes, lightly seasoned
🧀 Cheese Fresh mozzarella (buffalo preferred)
🌿 Topping Fresh basil leaves
🫒 Finish Olive oil drizzle
🔥 Cooking Wood-fired at ~900°F for 90 seconds

🍕 Chapter 1.4: The Margherita Experience

Enjoy it hot and fresh out of the oven. In Italy, it's often eaten with a knife and fork. Ideal pairings: sparkling water, wine, or light beer.
